# Grass Roots Cyber - Membership Agreement

## Purpose
This Membership Agreement affirms your participation in Grass Roots Cyber ("the Organization"), an unincorporated nonprofit association committed to cybersecurity education, mutual aid, and regenerative digital stewardship.

By signing this agreement, you acknowledge and support the mission, principles, and participatory nature of the Organization as outlined in the Statement of Organization.

## Member Commitments
As a member of Grass Roots Cyber, you commit to:

1. **Support the Mission**
   - Promote cyber resilience and community knowledge-sharing.
   - Uphold values aligned with Free/Libre/Open Source Software (FLOSS), equity, and digital rights.

2. **Participate in Open Governance**
   - Engage in decision-making processes based on Open Space Technology (OST).
   - Respect diverse perspectives and encourage community-led leadership.

3. **Act in Good Faith**
   - Contribute honestly and constructively.
   - Maintain a spirit of collaboration, accountability, and mutual respect.

## Rights of Members
- Access to all public resources, discussions, and educational events of the Organization.
- The ability to propose initiatives, lead working groups, and help shape the future of the community.
- The right to vote on key decisions and participate in OST sessions.

## Signing & Verification
This agreement may be signed through any of the following methods:
- GitHub Pull Request to the public membership registry.
- Submission through the Organization's `/sign` website page.
- Public upload to the Internet Archive referencing the Statement of Organization.
- Cryptographic signature tied to a verified email or key.
- Physical signature on a witnessed document.

## Voluntary & Revocable
- Membership is voluntary and may be ended by the member at any time.
- The Organization may remove members only in cases of demonstrable harm or repeated bad faith actions, and only through transparent, participatory review.

## A Living Agreement
This Membership Agreement may evolve over time, with updates proposed and accepted via open community processes. The most current version shall always be published publicly.

---
**To Join:** Sign this agreement using one of the approved methods. Your name, optional contact info, and timestamp will be added to the public membership list.
