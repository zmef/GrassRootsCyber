# Grass Roots Cyber - Statement of Organization

## Name
Grass Roots Cyber ("the Organization")

## Type
Unincorporated Nonprofit Association operating in the State of Illinois.

## Purpose
Grass Roots Cyber exists to promote cybersecurity awareness, education, and resilience at the community level, with a focus on Free/Libre/Open Source Software (FLOSS), digital stewardship, and regenerative technological practices.

## Membership
Membership is open to individuals and organizations that support the mission and principles of the Organization. Members may join by signing the Membership Agreement publicly or via verified consent using any approved distributed signing method.

Members are expected to:
- Uphold the values of cyber resilience, transparency, mutual aid, and ethical technology.
- Participate in community decision-making processes.

## Governance
Grass Roots Cyber operates using a **decentralized, asymmetric model** inspired by **Open Space Technology (OST)** principles. This means:
- Leadership and facilitation emerge from participation, not title.
- Decisions are made by those present and engaged.
- Meetings are guided by self-organization and mutual respect.

No hierarchical leadership structure is imposed; instead, the community governs itself through circles of responsibility and spontaneous contribution.

## Public Accessibility
This document shall be hosted publicly, mirrored across multiple platforms (e.g., GitHub Pages, Internet Archive), and shall remain freely viewable and forkable by any interested party.

All updates, edits, or additions to this document must be proposed and approved through open participation channels.

## Living Document
This Statement of Organization is a **living document**. It evolves as the needs and insights of the community evolve. All changes will be version-controlled and logged publicly.
